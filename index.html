My Photography Journey

When I was 14, my aunt gave me her starter camera as a gift to get me started on photography. I took a few pictures throughout high school, but everything really changed when she took my Senior Photos. I had never felt so confident or looked so good in pictures. That sparked my interest in being able to provide that same positive experience for other people.
